What I Built

A fully functional freight-factoring and banking operations console built on your exact tech stack: NestJS + Drizzle + Postgres on the backend, Next.js 15 App Router on the frontend. Real JWT auth, four RBAC roles, and end-to-end invoice factoring with integer-cents math and a double-entry FIDC ledger. No floats on any money path.

The demo seeded with three user roles so you can click through every screen: a carrier submitting invoices, an underwriter approving and disbursing advances, and an admin reviewing the ledger. All amounts stored as integer minor units throughout.

Core Features
📄

Invoice Factoring Lifecycle

Carrier submits invoice. Underwriter approves, system computes advance rate, fee, and reserve in integer cents. Advance disbursed. Carrier collects. Every state transition logged.

🏦

FIDC Double-Entry Ledger

Every factoring event posts correct debit/credit pairs. Chart of accounts with real balances. Admin ledger view shows full accounting trail. All amounts in integer minor units.

🔐

Identity and RBAC

Real JWT auth with four roles: admin, underwriter, carrier, driver. NestJS Guards on every protected route. Role-aware nav and action gating. Seeded demo credentials.
