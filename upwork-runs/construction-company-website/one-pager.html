Why this demo stands out

What Makes It Work

1
The video is done correctly
Same-origin MP4 file (no CORS issues, no broken embeds), autoplay with muted and playsinline attributes set, dark gradient overlay for legibility on every frame, and a JS error handler that falls back to the poster image if the video cannot load. Most "video background" demos break in production. This one does not.
2
Pricing transparency is a real differentiator
Most construction company websites hide their prices and say "call for a quote." The research is clear: firms that show ballpark pricing convert better and attract more qualified leads. The Services page gives real starting numbers for home building and excavation, and explains what each tier includes.
3
The gallery qualifies leads by service type
A homeowner researching a custom build, a commercial tenant doing a buildout, and an excavation buyer all have different needs. The filterable gallery lets each type of client self-identify, see only relevant work, and click into a project detail lightbox. This is the #1 conversion driver on construction marketing sites.
4
Bespoke design with no template feel
Charcoal black backgrounds, safety amber accent, Oswald condensed headlines, sharp button edges, and generous whitespace. No stock template shortcuts. The design system was built from scratch to match the visual language of established regional construction firms (Turner, Suffolk, Ryan Companies) without copying any of them.
5
Mobile-first and accessible
Responsive across mobile, tablet, and desktop. Navigation collapses into a hamburger drawer below 768px. WCAG 2.1 AA baseline: proper aria labels, keyboard navigation through the lightbox and forms, focus management, and reduced-motion support for all animations.
