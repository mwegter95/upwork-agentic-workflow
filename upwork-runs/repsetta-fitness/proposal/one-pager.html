How it works

The core capture loop, end to end

The same loop your product lives or dies on, built to feel fast and clean on a phone.

01

Today's program

A guided session (Push Day A) loads from the backend and seeds the logger with target exercises and sets.

02

Log + rest timer

Enter reps and weight per set. Each logged set auto-starts a live countdown ring with pause, plus fifteen seconds, and skip.

03

Save to a real API

Finishing posts the full session to a Flask service on my infrastructure. It persists, no fake data.

04

Progress

History, total volume, streak, and a volume-per-session chart, drawn from the data you just logged.
