Card Shopper
The vision

Recreate the floor of a card show on the web.

Walk a map of verified shops, find one you trust, and dig through a value box one card at a time. Curation is the spine: a vetted marketplace, not an open free-for-all, so collectors and founding sellers both feel safe moving inventory here.

Card Shopper
The buyer journey

From the map to the box to the buy, in one flow.

1. Browse the map

A US map with category-colored pins, drop banners, global search, and category tiles. Tap a pin to open a verified shop.

2. Dig the box

Flip through the value box or switch to grid. Search by player, team, or set. Filter by price, category, and grade.

3. Act on a card

Watchlist it, message the seller, make an offer with live status, or buy instantly. Follow the shop for its next drop.

The seller booth

A booth that charges one flat fee. Zero per-sale fees.

Sellers apply and are manually verified before they can list. Approved sellers get a dashboard with live stats: active listings, monthly sales, open offers, and followers. This is the pitch your founding sellers have been waiting for.

  • Manual verification gate, curation made visible
  • One flat monthly subscription, keep 100 percent of every sale
  • Live dashboard stats driven by real activity
  • Built to recruit your 10 to 20 founding sellers
05
Card Shopper
The AI-assisted upload

Four steps from a photo to a priced listing.

1. Photo

Seller snaps the card. The flow opens with a capture step.

2. Detect

CollX-style auto-detect fills player, set, year, and grade. Fields stay editable.

3. Price

A market-price suggestion appears. The seller accepts or overrides it.

4. Publish

Goes live as a standalone listing or drops straight into a value box.

The drop builder works the same way: fill the title, date, and teaser, watch a live preview, then fan it out to followers by in-app notification and email.
