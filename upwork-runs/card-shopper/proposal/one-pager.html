The problem and the vision

Collectors miss the show. Sellers resent the fees.

The magic of an in-person card show is tactile: you walk the floor, find a shop you trust, and dig through a value box one card at a time. Today's online marketplaces lose that feeling and take a cut of every sale on top of it.

Card Shopper rebuilds the experience for the web: a US map of manually verified shops, a value box you actually flip through, and a flat monthly subscription that lets sellers keep 100 percent of every sale. Curation is the spine. This is a vetted marketplace, not an open free-for-all, and that trust is what convinces founding sellers to move their inventory here.

The value-box flip (the signature)

Browse, search, and filter

Search inventory by player, team, or set. Filter by price and category, sort by price, grade, or newest. Results recompute live.

Buyer actions

Save to a watchlist, message a seller per listing, make an offer with pending, accepted, and declined states, or buy instantly.

Curation and trust gate

Sellers apply and are manually verified before they can list. The approval gate is built into the booth, so curation is visible.

Seller booth dashboard

Active listings, monthly sales, open offers, and follower count, all updating live from real actions in the prototype.

4-step AI-assisted upload

Photo, then CollX-style auto-detect of player, set, year, and grade, then a market-price suggestion you can accept or override, then publish standalone or into a value box.

Drops, follows, and notifications

Build a drop with a live preview, fan it out to followers by in-app notification and email, and follow shops to receive them.

Proposed architecture

Built on Bubble.io, wired to the services you named.

The prototype is a high-fidelity preview of the experience. The real product gets built on Bubble.io so you own a visual platform you can iterate on, with each integration handling exactly the job you specified.

Bubble.io core

The full web app and data model: users, shops, listings, value boxes, offers, messages, drops, follows, and watchlists. Mobile-responsive from day one.

Stripe subscriptions

True recurring billing for the flat monthly seller fee, not one-time charges, with zero fees on individual sales. Billing state gates seller listing.

Maps

Google Maps or Mapbox plugin for the pin-based map of verified shops, color-coded by category.

CollX API

Photo-to-details auto-detection in the upload flow: player, set, year, and grade, plus a market-price reference for the seller.

Klaviyo

Drop announcement emails that fan out to a shop's followers, paired with in-app notifications.

Median.co

Wraps the responsive web app for the App Store and Google Play later, with no rebuild needed.
