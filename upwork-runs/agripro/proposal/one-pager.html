How It Works

1
Log in as any role Procurement, Quality, Warehouse, or Management. Each sees a different dashboard, nav scope, and action set.
2
Scan a lab certificate Drop a grain certificate image into the OCR upload zone. The classifier identifies the document type; extracted values populate the inspection record automatically.
3
Review anomalies The rule engine compares extracted values against crop-specific reference bands and surfaces any out-of-range readings with severity level and explanation, before they reach the approval step.
4
Advance the approval pipeline Each inspection moves through 6 stages (Draft, Field, Lab Review, QA Approval, Procurement Approval, Warehouse Allocation) with Approve / Flag / Reject controls at every gate.
5
Export and audit Generate a PDF or Excel report from the dashboard view. Every stage change and approval action is written to the audit log with actor, role, timestamp, and record ID.
